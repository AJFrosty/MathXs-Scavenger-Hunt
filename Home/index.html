Who We Are

MathXs started with a simple goal: make math feel possible for everyone.

Math support that feels human

MathXs Education was built to support students across Antigua & Barbuda with calm, consistent, confidence-first learning. We focus on clarity, practice that sticks, and guidance that actually helps.

It began as a small community effort — helping friends, classmates, and younger students — and grew into something bigger: a place where students can improve without feeling judged.

  • Friendly explanations, not pressure
  • Practice that builds real confidence
  • Community events like Pi Day + challenges
