About Me

I'm Ajante Fraser, the founder of MathXs, a platform initially created to support secondary school students in CSEC mathematics. Currently I am a student at the University of the West Indies: Five Islands Campus, my passion for mathematics extends beyond the classroom. As an advocate for the subject, I find fulfillment in promoting its importance and impact. MathXs is a testament to my commitment to making mathematics accessible and enjoyable for students, fostering a positive learning environment.
